Steven Paul Jobs born in San Francisco, California to Abdulfattah 'John' Jandali and Joanne Carole Schieble

1955 personal 90% confidence

Adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs in Mountain View, California

Adoption experience profoundly influenced his perfectionism and need for control

1967 personal 85% confidence

Enrolled at Homestead High School in Cupertino, California

Beginning of formal education in heart of Silicon Valley

1971 personal 90% confidence

Met Steve Wozniak through mutual friend Bill Fernandez

Beginning of partnership that would create Apple Computer

1972 career 90% confidence

Enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon

Expensive liberal arts education that influenced his aesthetic sensibilities

1972 personal 90% confidence

Dropped out of Reed College after one semester but continued auditing classes

Pattern of unconventional approach to formal education and authority

1974 career 90% confidence

Hired as technician at Atari, Inc.

First significant job in technology industry, influenced design philosophy

Traveled to India seeking spiritual enlightenment

Spiritual journey that reinforced interest in Eastern philosophy and minimalism

1975 career 85% confidence

Attended first meeting of Homebrew Computer Club

Exposure to personal computer revolution and entrepreneurial opportunities

Co-founded Apple Computer Company with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne

Birth of company that would revolutionize personal computing

Released Apple I computer

First commercial product establishing Apple in personal computer market

Launched Apple II computer at West Coast Computer Faire

Revolutionary product that established personal computer industry

1980 business 95% confidence

Apple Computer went public with largest IPO in history at that time

1997 career 95% confidence

Returned to Apple as interim CEO after company acquired NeXT

Dramatic return to save struggling Apple and begin greatest comeback in business history

Launched iMac computer

Launched original iPod
